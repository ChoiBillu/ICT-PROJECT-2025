❄️ Cold Chain Service

About This Service

Our Cold Chain service uses state-of-the-art refrigerated trucks (reefers) to transport temperature-sensitive goods. We maintain strict temperature control throughout the entire journey to ensure your products arrive fresh and safe.

What We Transport

  • Fresh fruits and vegetables
  • Dairy products (milk, cheese, yogurt)
  • Frozen foods and ice cream
  • Meat and poultry products
  • Pharmaceuticals and medicines
  • Seafood and fish
  • Beverages and juices

Our Features

  • Real-time temperature monitoring
  • GPS tracking for all shipments
  • 24/7 temperature alerts
  • Certified cold chain handling

Temperature Control

Frozen Storage

Temperature: -18°C to -25°C

Perfect for frozen foods, ice cream, and frozen meat products.

Chilled Storage

Temperature: 2°C to 8°C

Ideal for fresh produce, dairy products, and most perishable items.

Controlled Room Temperature

Temperature: 15°C to 25°C

For products that need stable temperature but not freezing.
