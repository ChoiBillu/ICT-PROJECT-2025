About This Service

Our Heavy Transport service specializes in moving large industrial equipment, construction machinery, and oversized cargo. We have a fleet of specialized trucks designed to handle weights up to 50 tons safely and efficiently.

What We Transport

  • Construction machinery and equipment
  • Industrial generators and transformers
  • Large containers and shipping crates
  • Heavy manufacturing equipment
  • Agricultural machinery
  • Oversized vehicles and boats

Our Fleet

We operate a modern fleet of heavy-duty trucks including:

  • Flatbed trucks (up to 30 tons capacity)
  • Lowboy trailers (for extra heavy loads)
  • Step deck trailers (for tall equipment)
  • Specialized hydraulic trailers
